Movie Review...!

Baby



Rating:4.5/5

Review

Baby is a take on modern-day relationships, and director Sai Rajesh sheds light on the chosen topic through three beautifully written characters which are diverse in their own way.

Summary
  • Viraj ashwin made his debut with Anaganaga o prema katha.He was known to telugu audience with Thank You Brother which got good response from public, this was straight ott release, due to pandemic.
  • Viraj also known to national, international audience because of his short film "Manasanamaha" which has got Guinness record as highest awards international wide.
Draw backs
  • there's a lot of inappropriate stuff on display because the characters themselves are flawed and toxic.
  • Only drawback is the length and it gets dragged towards end. The walking scenes could have been edited.

Result:

'Baby' secured the 10th spot among the highest-grossing Telugu films of 2023.
